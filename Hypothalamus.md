- "CPU" of [[Endocrine System|endocrine system]]

- Located under the **thalamus**
- Primarily influences activity in the [[Pituitary Gland|pituitary gland]] through [[peptide hormones]]
- Interacts with **[[anterior pituitary]]** through the hypophyseal [[Portal System]]
